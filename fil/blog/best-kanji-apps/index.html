5 Pinakamahusay na Apps para Matuto ng Kanji sa 2026

Nagdesisyon kang matuto ng kanji. Magandang pagpili—pero masamang timing para sa iyong mental health. Kailangan mong kabisaduhin ang mahigit 2,000 na karakter para lang makabasa ng dyaryo, kaya kailangan mo ng magandang digital na kasama. Pero aling app ang pipiliin sa dami ng available? Ito ang tapat na paghahambing.

Ano ang hahanapin sa kanji app

  • Spaced Repetition (SRS) — Ang tanging scientifically proven na paraan para sa long-term memory
  • Iba't ibang exercises — Reading, meaning, listening, writing
  • JLPT coverage — Mula N5 hanggang N1
  • Built-in motivation — Streaks, goals, rewards
  • Abot-kayang presyo

1. Duolingo

Verdict: Maganda para magsimula, hindi sapat para sa kanji.

Pros: Attractive interface, libre, perfect para sa beginners. Cons: Hindi kanji ang focus, walang dedicated SRS, hanggang ~N4 lang.

2. Anki

Verdict: Sobrang powerful, pero kailangan ng master's degree sa configuration.

Pros: Isa sa pinakamahusay na SRS algorithms, fully customizable, libre sa desktop at Android. Cons: Luma ang interface, matarik na learning curve, iOS $25, walang gamification.

3. WaniKani

Verdict: Excellent method, premium price, English lang.

Pros: Radical system, creative mnemonics, structured path. Cons: ~$9/buwan, English lang, walang official app.

4. Kanji Study

Verdict: Magandang dictionary, hindi gaanong effective bilang active learning tool.

Pros: Complete database, stroke order animations, offline, one-time purchase. Cons: Parang dictionary, basic quizzes, Android lang.

5. Kanjidon

Verdict: Modern approach—serious gamification meets scientific SRS.

Pros

  • 8 types ng quiz—meaning, on/kun reading, listening, writing
  • SM-2 algorithm—scientifically validated SRS system
  • Mag-collect ng kanji parang cards
  • Real-time PvP battles—rank mula E hanggang S
  • 21 languages—matuto sa sariling wika
  • Full coverage—mula kana hanggang JLPT N1
  • Libre na may complete basic features
  • Native iOS at Android apps, gumagana offline

Cons

  • Hindi gumagamit ng narrative mnemonics
  • PvP battles kailangan ng internet
  • Relatively new app
