Kanji vs Chu Han Trung Quoc: Giai Thich Su Khac Biet

"Neu toi hoc kanji Nhat, toi co doc duoc tieng Trung khong?" Day la cau hoi pho bien, va cau tra loi la... phuc tap. Kanji bat nguon tu chu Han, nhung sau nhieu the ky phat trien rieng, co nhung khac biet dang ke.

Nguon goc chung

Kanji (, nghia den "chu Han") duoc nhap tu Trung Quoc sang Nhat Ban giua the ky thu 4 va thu 6 sau Cong nguyen. Vao thoi diem do, chung gan nhu giong het chu Han co dien.

Tuy nhien, tu do, ba dieu da thay doi:

  1. Nhat Ban phat trien cac bien the rieng
  2. Trung Quoc don gian hoa nhieu chu (thap nien 1950-60)
  3. Nghia phat trien khac nhau

Khac biet ve nghia

Ngay ca khi chu giong nhau, nghia co the khac:

(tegami)

  • Tieng Nhat: thu (gui qua buu dien)
  • Tieng Trung: giay ve sinh

(benkyo)

  • Tieng Nhat: hoc tap
  • Tieng Trung: ep buoc ai do lam gi

(daijobu)

  • Tieng Nhat: khong sao, on ca
  • Tieng Trung: nguoi dan ong vi dai dung cam

(hashiru)

  • Tieng Nhat: chay
  • Tieng Trung: di bo

Khac biet ve cach doc

Day la su khac biet lon nhat. Kanji Nhat co hai loai cach doc:

On'yomi ()

Cach doc "Trung Quoc", bat nguon tu phat am Trung Quoc co. Nhung chu y: no dua tren tieng Trung 1.500 nam truoc, nen rat khac voi tieng Trung hien dai.

Kun'yomi ()

Cach doc "Nhat Ban", dua tren tu ban dia Nhat. Dieu nay khong ton tai trong tieng Trung.

Vi du:

  • On'yomi Nhat: (san)
  • Kun'yomi Nhat: (yama)
  • Quan thoai Trung: shan
  • Quang Dong: saan1

Toi co the doc tieng Trung neu biet kanji khong?

Cau tra loi trung thuc:

  • Phon the Trung Quoc (Dai Loan): Ban co the hieu ~50-60% nghia chung cua van ban viet, nhung khong phat am duoc
  • Gian the Trung Quoc (Trung Quoc): Ban co the hieu ~30-40%, nhieu chu khac nhau
  • Noi: Khong. Cach phat am hoan toan khac nhau

Nen hoc cai nao?

Tuy thuoc vao muc tieu cua ban:

  • Muon song/lam viec o Nhat? - Kanji
  • Muon giao tiep voi 1,4 ty nguoi Trung Quoc? - Gian the
  • Quan tam den van hoa co dien va Dai Loan? - Phon the

Tin vui: hoc mot cai se giup hoc cac cai khac. Nen tang cau truc (bo thu, thanh phan) la giong nhau.
