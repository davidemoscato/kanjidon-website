Ung Dung Kanji Mien Phi: Tot Nhat 2026

Ban muon hoc kanji nhung khong muon ton nhieu tien cho cac goi dang ky? Tin vui: co nhung ung dung mien phi tuyet voi (hoac gan nhu mien phi) de hoc chu Nhat. Day la so sanh trung thuc cac lua chon hien co.

Can tim gi o ung dung mien phi

Khong phai tat ca ung dung "mien phi" deu nhu nhau. Mot so hoan toan mien phi, mot so la freemium (mien phi voi gioi han). Day la nhung gi can danh gia:

  • Bao nhieu noi dung thuc su mien phi? - Chi N5 hay den N1?
  • Co gioi han hang ngay khong? - Ban co the hoc tuy thich khong?
  • Tinh nang quan trong co mien phi khong? - SRS, quiz, offline?
  • Quang cao phien toai? - Quang cao moi 30 giay?

Nhung ung dung kanji mien phi tot nhat

1. Anki (Desktop + Android)

Chi phi: Mien phi tren desktop va Android, $24.99 tren iOS

Loai: Flashcard SRS hoan toan tuy chinh

Uu diem:

  • 100% mien phi tren desktop va Android
  • Mot trong nhung thuat toan SRS tot nhat
  • Bo the chia se tu cong dong
  • Hoan toan tuy chinh

Nhuoc diem:

  • Giao dien cu, khong truc quan
  • Can thoi gian de cau hinh
  • Khong co game hoa
  • Dat tren iOS ($24.99)

Phu hop cho: Nguoi dung ky thuat thich tuy chinh moi thu.

2. Duolingo

Chi phi: Mien phi voi quang cao, Super $7.99/thang

Loai: Khoa hoc ngon ngu chung bao gom kanji

Uu diem:

  • Hoan toan mien phi (voi quang cao)
  • Giao dien dep, game hoa
  • Hoan hao cho nguoi moi bat dau

Nhuoc diem:

  • Kanji khong phai trong tam
  • Pham vi han che (~N4)
  • SRS khong toi uu cho kanji
  • Quang cao thuong xuyen o ban mien phi

Phu hop cho: Nguoi muon bat dau voi tieng Nhat noi chung.

3. Takoboto

Chi phi: Mien phi (chi Android)

Loai: Tu dien tieng Nhat voi flashcard

Uu diem:

  • Hoan toan mien phi, khong quang cao
  • Tu dien offline day du
  • Flashcard tich hop

Nhuoc diem:

  • Chi Android
  • Tu dien nhieu hon ung dung hoc
  • SRS co ban
  • Khong game hoa

Phu hop cho: Nguoi tim tu dien mien phi kem flashcard.

4. Kanji Study

Chi phi: Mien phi (N5), $12.99 mo khoa tat ca

Loai: Co so du lieu kanji voi quiz

Uu diem:

  • N5 hoan toan mien phi
  • Co so du lieu day du, chi tiet
  • Hoat hinh net but
  • Mua mot lan (khong dang ky)

Nhuoc diem:

  • Chi Android
  • Chi N5 mien phi
  • Tham khao nhieu hon hoc chu dong
  • Quiz co ban

Phu hop cho: Nguoi muon tai lieu tham khao kanji chi tiet.

5. Kanjidon

Chi phi: Mien phi voi tinh nang day du, Premium tuy chon

Loai: Ung dung game hoa voi SRS va tran dau PvP

Uu diem:

  • Tat ca tinh nang co ban mien phi
  • Thuat toan SM-2 (SRS khoa hoc)
  • 8 loai quiz bao gom
  • Bao quat day du: kana - N1
  • Tran dau PvP mien phi
  • Ho tro 21 ngon ngu
  • Hoat dong offline
  • Khong quang cao

Nhuoc diem:

  • Mot so tinh nang nang cao can Premium
  • Ung dung tuong doi moi

Phu hop cho: Nguoi muon ung dung hien dai, day du ma khong tra phi.

Nen chon ung dung nao?

Neu ban co PC va Android: Anki cuc ky manh me va hoan toan mien phi (nhung hay chuan bi dau tu thoi gian cau hinh).

Neu ban la nguoi moi hoan toan: Duolingo hoan hao cho vai tuan dau, sau do chuyen sang ung dung chuyen biet hon.

Neu ban muon ung dung hien dai hoat dong ngay: Kanjidon cung cap SRS khoa hoc, 8 loai quiz va tran dau PvP - tat ca mien phi, khong can cai dat.

"Thu thuat" cua ung dung mien phi

Day la su that: ung dung hoan toan mien phi thuong co gioi han an hoac can hang gio cai dat. Ung dung "freemium" duoc thiet ke tot, nhu Kanjidon, cung cap tang mien phi thuc su huu ich.

Voi Kanjidon ban co the:

  • Hoc tat ca kanji tu kana den N1
  • Su dung he thong SRS day du
  • Lam tat ca loai quiz
  • Thach dau nguoi choi khac trong tran dau
  • Hoc offline

Tat ca khong tra gi va khong quang cao. Premium mo khoa tinh nang bo sung (nhu goi thuong va thong ke nang cao), nhung khong can thiet de hoc.

Bat dau mien phi ngay hom nay

Khong con ly do gi: ung dung kanji tot nhat la mien phi hoac co phien ban mien phi day du. Tai Kanjidon va bat dau hoc trong 5 phut.
