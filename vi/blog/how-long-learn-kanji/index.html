Hoc Kanji Mat Bao Lau?

"Hoc kanji mat bao lau?" la cau hoi pho bien nhat cua nguoi hoc tieng Nhat. Cau tra loi trung thuc la: tuy thuoc vao nhieu yeu to. Nhung dung lo - trong bai viet nay, toi se cho ban thoi gian thuc te dua tren du lieu thuc.

Nhung con so ban can biet

Truoc khi noi ve thoi gian, hay lam ro ban can bao nhieu kanji:

  • JLPT N5 - ~100 kanji (cap do co ban)
  • JLPT N4 - ~300 kanji (so cap)
  • JLPT N3 - ~650 kanji (trung cap)
  • JLPT N2 - ~1.000 kanji (nang cao)
  • JLPT N1 - ~2.136 kanji (thanh thao)

2.136 kanji o cap N1 la joyo kanji - nhung chu duoc su dung hang ngay o Nhat Ban. Biet nhung chu nay co nghia la ban co the doc bao, sach va van ban chinh thuc.

Day la uoc tinh dua tren 30 phut hoc moi ngay voi phuong phap hieu qua (SRS):

Luu y quan trong: thoi gian nay gia dinh ban dang su dung he thong lap lai cach quang (SRS). Neu khong dung SRS, thoi gian co the gap doi hoac gap ba.

Tai sao phuong phap quan trong hon thoi gian

Hai hoc vien co the hoc cung so gio nhung co ket qua hoan toan khac nhau. Su khac biet nam o phuong phap.

Phuong phap khong hieu qua (tranh nhung dieu nay)

  • Chep kanji bang tay khong he thong - Chi nho ngan han
  • Flashcard thu cong khong SRS - Lang phi thoi gian vao kanji da biet
  • Chi nhan dien thu dong - Ban khong hoc cach tao ra chu dong
  • Hoc khong co ngu canh - Kanji doc lap de quen

Phuong phap hieu qua (khuyen nghi)

  • Lap lai cach quang (SRS) - On tap moi kanji vao thoi diem toi uu
  • Quiz chu dong da dang - Nghia, cach doc, nghe, viet
  • Tu ghep - Hoc kanji trong ngu canh
  • Deu dan moi ngay - Tot hon 20 phut moi ngay hon 3 gio cuoi tuan

Van de dong luc

Tro ngai thuc su khong phai la thoi gian - ma la su kien tri. Hau het moi nguoi bo cuoc trong vai tuan dau tien. Day la ly do:

  • Kanji deu trong giong nhau luc dau
  • Tien bo cham va kho nhin thay
  • Viec hoc tro nen nham chan va lap di lap lai
  • Khong co phan hoi tuc thi ve tien do

Giai phap? Mot he thong lam cho viec hoc hap dan va cho thay tien bo mot cach cu the.

Mat bao lau voi Kanjidon?

Voi 20-30 phut moi ngay tren Kanjidon, day la thoi gian thuc te:

  • N5 (100 kanji) - 4-6 tuan
  • N4 (300 kanji) - 2-3 thang
  • N3 (650 kanji) - 5-6 thang
  • N2 (1.000 kanji) - 10-12 thang
  • N1 (2.136 kanji) - 18-20 thang

Meo de tang toc

  1. Hoc moi ngay - Su kien tri thang cuong do
  2. Dung bo on tap - He thong SRS chi hoat dong neu ban theo no
  3. Tan dung thoi gian chet - 5 phut tren tau dien ngam, xep hang, nghi trua
  4. Dat muc tieu hang ngay - Vi du: 5 kanji moi + tat ca bai on
  5. Dung voi - Tot hon hoc 3 kanji that ky hon 10 kanji qua loa

Bat dau ngay hom nay

Thoi diem tot nhat de bat dau la mot nam truoc. Thoi diem tot thu hai la bay gio. Kanjidon mien phi voi tat ca tinh nang co ban. Tai ve va trong 10 phut ban se hoc duoc kanji dau tien.
