Thay The Duolingo Cho Tieng Nhat: Hoc Kanji Hieu Qua

Duolingo rat tuyet voi de bat dau hoc tieng Nhat. Giao dien hap dan, bai hoc ngan gon, va con cu xanh nhac ban hoc moi ngay. Nhung sau vai tuan, ban nhan ra mot van de: kanji khong vao dau duoc. Va do khong phai loi cua ban.

Van de kanji cua Duolingo

Duolingo duoc thiet ke de hoc ngoai ngu noi chung. No hoat dong rat tot voi tieng Tay Ban Nha, tieng Phap hay tieng Duc - nhung ngon ngu co bang chu cai tuong tu tieng Anh. Nhung tieng Nhat lai khac: no co ba he thong chu viet (hiragana, katakana, kanji) va can ghi nho hon 2.000 ky tu chi de doc bao.

Day la nhung han che cu the cua Duolingo voi kanji:

  • Khong co he thong SRS chuyen biet - Kanji xuat hien ngau nhien, khong theo lap lai cach quang khoa hoc
  • Pham vi han che - Chi dat khoang trinh do JLPT N4, thieu N3, N2 va N1
  • Khong luyen viet - Ban khong bao gio hoc cach viet chu
  • Cach doc khong day du - Thuong thieu on'yomi va kun'yomi
  • Thieu ngu canh - It vi du ve tu ghep

Ban thuc su can gi de hoc kanji

Nghien cuu khoa hoc da chi ro: de ghi nho hang ngan ky tu, ban can mot he thong lap lai cach quang (SRS) hien thi moi kanji vao dung thoi diem - khong qua som, khong qua muon.

Ban cung can:

  • Bai kiem tra da dang - Nghia, cach doc, nghe, viet
  • Bao quat day du JLPT - Tu N5 den N1
  • Dong luc duy tri - Game hoa thuc su hieu qua
  • Luyen tap chu dong - Khong chi nhan dien thu dong

Kanjidon: Ung dung duoc xay dung cho kanji

Kanjidon duoc tao ra dac biet de giai quyet van de nay. Day la ung dung chuyen ve kanji, voi he thong hoc tap dua tren khoa hoc va game hoa thong minh.

Kanjidon mang lai gi

  • Thuat toan SM-2 - Cung he thong SRS nhu Anki, da duoc khoa hoc xac nhan
  • 8 loai quiz - Nghia, cach doc on/kun, nghe, viet tay
  • Bao quat day du - Tu kana den JLPT N1 (2.136+ kanji)
  • Bo suu tap the - Moi kanji la mot the suu tap
  • Tran dau PvP - Thach dau nguoi hoc khac theo thoi gian thuc
  • 21 ngon ngu - Hoc bang tieng me de, khong chi tieng Anh
  • Hoat dong offline - Hoc ngay ca khi khong co mang

Khi nao dung Duolingo va khi nao dung Kanjidon

Dung Duolingo neu:

  • Ban bat dau tu so khong voi tieng Nhat
  • Ban muon hoc cau co ban va ngu phap
  • Ban cung quan tam den cac ngon ngu khac

Chuyen sang Kanjidon khi:

  • Kanji cua Duolingo khong vao dau
  • Ban muon chuan bi nghiem tuc cho JLPT
  • Ban can he thong SRS chuyen biet
  • Ban muon hoc ca cach viet chu

Su ket hop hoan hao

Thuc ra, Duolingo va Kanjidon khong phai doi thu: chung bo sung cho nhau. Ban co the dung Duolingo cho ngu phap va hoi thoai, va Kanjidon dac biet de thao kanji. Nhieu hoc vien dung ca hai ung dung voi ket qua tuyet voi.

Mien phi de thu

Kanjidon mien phi voi tat ca tinh nang co ban. Ban co the bat dau ngay ma khong can the tin dung, mo goi the dau tien, lam quiz, va tham chi thach dau nguoi choi khac. Trong 10 phut ban se biet no co phu hop voi minh khong.
