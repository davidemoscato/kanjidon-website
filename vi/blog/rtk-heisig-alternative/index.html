Thay The RTK: Hoc Kanji Bang Phuong Phap Hien Dai

"Remembering the Kanji" cua James Heisig la cach mang vao nam 1977. Nhung bay gio la 2026, va co nhung phuong phap ky thuat so hieu qua hon. Hay xem uu nhuoc diem cua RTK va cach ung dung hien dai co the cai thien trai nghiem.

RTK (Remembering the Kanji) la gi?

Remembering the Kanji la cuon sach cua James Heisig de xuat phuong phap ghi nho de hoc 2.136 joyo kanji. Y tuong cot loi la:

  1. Phan tich moi kanji thanh cac "nguyen to" (thanh phan thi giac)
  2. Tao cau chuyen ghi nho ket noi cac nguyen to voi nghia
  3. Hoc tat ca nghia truoc, cach doc sau

Vi du: Kanji (nghi ngoi) duoc phan tich thanh (nguoi) + (cay). Cau chuyen: "Mot nguoi tua vao cay dang nghi ngoi."

Diem manh cua RTK

  • Cach tiep can co he thong - Thu tu logic dua tren thanh phan
  • Ghi nho sang tao - Cau chuyen giup tri nho
  • Tap trung vao thanh phan - Ban hoc cach "doc" cau truc kanji
  • Day du - Bao quat tat ca 2.136 joyo kanji

Han che cua RTK

Mac du co gia tri, RTK co mot so van de vao nam 2026:

1. Khong co cach doc

RTK chi day nghia. Cach doc (on'yomi va kun'yomi) de lai cho "sau nay." Nhung khong co cach doc, ban khong the:

  • Doc tu tieng Nhat
  • Hieu tieng Nhat noi
  • Noi tieng Nhat

2. Chi tieng Anh

Cuon sach duoc viet bang tieng Anh. Neu tieng Anh khong phai tieng me de cua ban, ban phai:

  • Nghi bang ngon ngu thu ba
  • Dich cau chuyen ghi nho trong dau
  • Mat sac thai van hoa

3. Dinh dang sach

Sach giay vao nam 2026 co nghia la:

  • Khong co he thong SRS tich hop
  • Ban phai quan ly on tap thu cong
  • Khong the hoc de dang khi di chuyen
  • Khong co phan hoi tuc thi

4. Khong co ngu canh

Ban hoc kanji rieng le khong co tu ghep hoac cau. Dieu nay khien viec ap dung kien thuc vao doc thuc te kho khan.

5. Dong luc kho duy tri

Doc sach 500+ trang can ky luat sat. Nhieu nguoi bo cuoc truoc khi hoan thanh.

Kanjidon giu lai gi tu RTK

Chung toi khong bo di nhung y tuong hay cua Heisig:

Tap trung vao thanh phan (bo thu)

Kanjidon giup ban tu nhien nhan ra cac mau trong kanji. Theo thoi gian, nao cua ban hoc cach tu dong phan tich moi ky tu.

Hoc tien bo

Kanji duoc sap xep theo cap do JLPT, tu don gian nhat den phuc tap nhat. Ban hoc thanh phan co ban truoc nhung thanh phan nang cao.

Tri nho thi giac

He thong the suu tap tan dung tri nho thi giac, lam cho moi kanji doc nhat va de nho.

Kanjidon them gi

Hoc toan dien

Voi 8 loai quiz, ban hoc moi thu ve moi kanji:

  • Nghia - Kanji
  • Cach doc On'yomi
  • Cach doc Kun'yomi
  • Nghe - Kanji
  • Viet tay
  • Tu ghep

SRS khoa hoc (SM-2)

Thuat toan tu dong tinh toan khi nao can on tap moi kanji. Khong bao gio quen nua, khong bao gio lang phi thoi gian vao nhung chu da biet.

Game hoa tao dong luc

  • Bo suu tap the - Moi kanji la mot the suu tap
  • Tran dau PvP - Thach dau hoc vien khac theo thoi gian thuc
  • Cap do va hang - Tu 1 den 50, hang tu E den S
  • Nhiem vu hang ngay - Muc tieu duy tri thoi quen

Tinh di dong

Hoc o bat cu dau: tren tau dien ngam, nghi trua, xep hang. Hoat dong ca offline.

Cho nhung ai van thich RTK

Neu ban da bat dau RTK va no dang hieu qua voi ban, ban khong can bo no. Ban co the dung Kanjidon nhu bo sung de:

  • Hoc cach doc ma RTK khong day
  • On tap voi SRS thay vi thu cong
  • Luyen nghe va viet
  • Them chut vui vao qua trinh hoc

Mien phi de thu

Kanjidon mien phi voi tat ca tinh nang co ban. Tai ve, thu quiz dau tien, mo vai goi the. Trong 10 phut ban se biet cach tiep can hien dai nay co phu hop voi minh khong.
