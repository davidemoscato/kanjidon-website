Delete Your Account

GDPR Article 17 - Right to Erasure

How to Delete Your Account

You have two options to delete your Kanjidon account and all associated personal data:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account:

  1. Open the Kanjidon app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the confirmation message
  6. Enter your password or authenticate to confirm
  7. Tap "Confirm Delete"

Processing Time: Immediate deletion upon confirmation.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer written confirmation:

  1. Send an email to info@kanjidon.com
  2. Use subject line: [DELETE ACCOUNT]
  3. Include the email address associated with your Kanjidon account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request

Processing Time: Maximum 30 days as per GDPR requirements.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (username, email, avatar)
  • All learning progress and quiz statistics
  • Your kanji collection and card inventory
  • Mission progress and achievements
  • Streak data and Tama coin balance
  • Friend relationships and friend codes
  • Battle statistics and ranking history
  • Ganbatte history (your identity is anonymized)
  • Push notification tokens (FCM)
  • Custom deck configurations
  • All stored preferences and settings

Data Retention Exceptions

Some data may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Purchase receipts 10 years Italian tax law requirements
Open user reports Until resolved Safety investigation
Ban records 7 years Legal defense purposes
Anonymized analytics 2 years Aggregate statistics only

This retained data is either anonymized or required by law. It cannot be used to identify you.

Deletion Timeline

Phase Timeline Description
Immediate 0-24 hours Account deactivated, removed from public leaderboards
Primary Deletion 7 days All personal data removed from active systems
Backup Purge 30 days Data removed from backup systems

Before You Delete

Please Consider:

  • This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and progress cannot be recovered.
  • Cancel your subscription first. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Manage subscriptions via App Store/Play Store.
  • Export your data. Before deleting, you can export your learning progress and data via Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Export Data.
  • Purchased items are not refundable. In-app purchases cannot be refunded after account deletion.

Alternative: Data Export

If you want to keep your learning data before deleting:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Export Data
  2. Choose your format (JSON, CSV, or XML)
  3. Download your complete data package

Your export includes: learning progress, quiz statistics, collection data, friend list, and all personal information stored.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As per Article 17 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of all your personal data
  • Right of Access: Obtain a copy of your data before deletion
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing

We are committed to honoring these rights within the legal timeframes.

Confirmation

After processing your deletion request:

  • You will receive an email confirming the deletion
  • The confirmation includes the types of data deleted
  • Any data retained for legal reasons is itemized

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

Email: info@kanjidon.com

Subject: [DELETE ACCOUNT] or [PRIVACY]

Response Time: Within 5 business days

For urgent privacy matters, use subject: [URGENT PRIVACY]
