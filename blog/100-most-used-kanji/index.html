The 100 Most Used Kanji in Daily Life in Japan

10 min min read

Not all kanji are equally important. Some appear everywhere: on signs, in menus, at stations. Here are the 100 most frequent kanji that will be immediately useful in Japan.

How to Use This List

Don't try to memorize all 100 kanji at once. Start with 5 per day, use spaced repetition, and learn each kanji in the context of words. In 3-4 weeks you'll have a solid foundation for getting around in Japan.
