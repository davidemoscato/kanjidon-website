Frequency is a practical filter: the characters you see every day are the ones that give the fastest return. Learn them with words and short sentences, not as isolated drawings.

लवकर लक्षात घ्यायची उदाहरणे

  • 日 (day, sun)
  • 人 (person)
  • 年 (year)
  • 大 (big)
  • 小 (small)
  • 中 (inside, middle)
  • 本 (book, origin)
  • 学 (study)
